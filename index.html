About ExitFinders

ExitFinders is dedicated to helping business owners find the perfect buyer for their company. Whether you're planning retirement or exploring new ventures, we provide expert support every step of the way. Our mission is to make your exit smooth, profitable, and stress-free.

Our Team

We are a team of finance majors at Boise State University—Caden Economos, Tyler Afdahl, and Luke Winchester, who specialize in helping business owners connect with the right buyers. Drawing on rigorous financial analysis, market valuation techniques, and hands‑on deal structuring experience, we guide you through every phase of the exit process. We strive ensure your company’s value is maximized and the transition is seamless.

Our Services

Buyer Matchmaking: We connect you with pre-screened, serious buyers.

Valuation Services: Get an accurate and fair market value for your business.

Negotiation Support: Our experts help you navigate complex negotiations with ease.
